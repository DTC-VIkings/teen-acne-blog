## General Style Notes for All Images

- **Color palette**: Match the blog's teal (#02838d) accent color where possible in illustrations
- **Background**: Clean white or very light gray for product/flat lay shots
- **Lighting**: Soft, diffused, professional — never harsh or clinical-feeling
- **Tone**: Medical authority meets warmth and approachability
- **Avoid**: Graphic/disturbing close-ups, brand names on products, unrealistic skin, stock photo feel
- **Resolution**: Generate at highest available resolution, will be displayed at various sizes
- **Format**: Save all as .png for best quality
